Five prompt workflows.

Market sizing · Customer segmentation · SWOT · Financial modeling · Interview synthesis. Here's one in full.

Market Sizing TAM / SAM / SOM

Forces the model to do what it's bad at by default — citing sources, grading evidence, flagging weak assumptions.

You are a consulting analyst. Estimate the TAM, SAM, and SOM for [PRODUCT]
in [GEOGRAPHY], serving [ICP], priced at [PRICE].

Use both methods:
1. Top-down: cite industry reports, name them explicitly.
2. Bottom-up: # of target customers × adoption rate × ACV.

Output format:
- Numbered assumption table: assumption | value | source | grade (A/B/C)
- TAM / SAM / SOM with the math shown
- Flag the 3 assumptions most likely to break the estimate and why.

If you cannot source a number, label it ESTIMATED and explain the logic.
Do not invent reports or statistics.
